CDLI Markov
This toy will generate a new fake atf-styled text from a dump of the transliterated texts from the CDLI project using a very simple Markov chain. Markov chains work by assigning to each word in a text a list of all the words that follow it, e.g. "United" â†’ ("States", "Airlines"). The text generator starts with a single word and then selects a random word that follows the first, and so on... until the desired length is reached. It can be a remarkably powerful way to generate new fake texts that resemble their source material, it's been used to generate fake versions of Trump to the Bible (and everything inbetween).

Load the data first and then click "Generate Text", it's quite slow loading the large text file, but once it's done the "Generate Text" button should be active.
